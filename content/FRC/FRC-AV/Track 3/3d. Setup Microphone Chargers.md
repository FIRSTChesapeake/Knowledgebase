Equipment needed: Microphone charger cradle
1. Retrieve the charger cradle from the drawers of the [[FRC/FRC-AV/index#Equipment Locations and Storage Descriptions|Storage Case]]. 
2. Place the cradle on one of the table surfaces, and plug it into the power strip. 
3. Place unused microphones in the charger. Charge all in-use microphones at least once a day, during lunch or overnight. 
Stage 3d is complete.
